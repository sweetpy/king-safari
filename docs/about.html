Our Story

King Safari & Car Hire was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional transportation services that exceed expectations. With over a decade of experience in Tanzania's tourism and transport industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, luxury, and outstanding customer service.

From humble beginnings with a single vehicle, we have grown into Tanzania's premier car hire and safari service provider, serving thousands of satisfied customers from around the world.
