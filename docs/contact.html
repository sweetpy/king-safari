Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance, especially during peak season. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability.

Do you provide airport pickup services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 airport pickup and drop-off services from Kilimanjaro International Airport, Arusha Airport, and Julius Nyerere International Airport in Dar es Salaam.

Are your vehicles insured?

All our vehicles are fully insured with comprehensive coverage. We maintain all required licenses and certifications for your safety and peace of mind.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (USD/TZS), mobile money, bank transfers, and major credit cards. Payment terms can be discussed based on your booking requirements.
