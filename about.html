About King Safari

King Safari & Car Hire offers premium transportation services across Tanzania. With over a decade of experience, we pride ourselves on providing immaculate vehicles and knowledgeable local guides.

Our mission is to deliver safe and reliable mobility solutions while maintaining global standards of service. Whether you need an airport pickup or a week-long safari, our team ensures every trip is hassle-free.

  • Professional drivers and multilingual guides
  • Well-maintained fleet for all group sizes
  • Customizable itineraries and tour packages

We operate from Arusha but serve clients across Kilimanjaro, Moshi, and Dar es Salaam.
